📜 How to Play

  • Goal: Close as many numbered cards as possible. Your score = sum of open cards (lowest wins!).
  • Step 1: Click Roll Dice to roll all four dice and get a sum.
  • Step 2: Click Show Board. Select any combination of cards that adds up exactly to your roll.
  • Step 3: Click Confirm to strike those numbers out.
  • Step 4: If no combination is possible, click Confirm with nothing selected to end your turn.
Example: Roll a 10? You can close (10), (9+1), (7+2+1), or (6+4)!
